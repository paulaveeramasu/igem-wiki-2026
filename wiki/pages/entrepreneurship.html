Entrepreneurship

Value Proposition

Metlock isn't just another water treatment system - it's a dual-purpose zinc recovery + environmental compliance tool. It allows mining and metal processing companies to:

  • Recover additional zinc from wastewater and tailings streams that would otherwise be lost.
  • Reduce environmental discharge penalties by sequestering zinc before effluent release.
  • Meet tightening ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) standards and demonstrate sustainable practices to regulators, investors, and communities.
  • Turn “waste treatment” into a revenue stream.

Target Customers

Primary Market:

  • Mining companies extracting zinc (or polymetallic ores with zinc byproducts).
  • Zinc smelters and refiners.

Secondary Market:

  • Industrial wastewater treatment plants dealing with heavy metal discharges.
  • Municipal/regional water authorities in mining-heavy regions.
  • Companies aiming for ESG certifications who need “green” water treatment solutions.
  • Electronics/chip manufacturers who need specific zinc concentrations for batching during production.

Possible Revenue Streams

Hardware + Installation:

  • Sell the Metlock system (bioreactor + media + strains) as a capital investment with installation fees.
  • Tiered system sizes (small, medium, large) depending on wastewater throughput.

Recurring Service Contracts:

  • Maintenance & Monitoring Subscriptions: Offer monthly or annual contracts for upkeep, microbial recharge, and system optimization.
  • Performance-based Contracts: Customers pay a % of the value of zinc recovered (aligns incentives, makes Metlock low-risk to adopt).

Licensing the Technology

  • Partner with large industrial equipment providers or water treatment companies and license Metlock tech. Revenue through royalties.

Sale of Secondary Zinc:

  • Operate Metlock systems as a toll recovery service (e.g., on-site or regional plants) where Metlock recovers zinc from wastewater, sells it on the metals market, and shares profits with the client.

Competitive Advantage

  • Unlike traditional bioreactors, Metlock is integrated into the zinc flotation circuit rather than just an afterthought treatment.
  • Higher zinc recovery rates = more revenue for clients and investor interest.
  • Lower environmental liability costs = less risk for clients and compliance with regulations.
  • Strong ESG/green branding = marketable advantage.
